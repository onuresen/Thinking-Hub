One connected city, six lenses. Buildings are tools — the Hub Pages district holds Thinking Hub's own tools (lit by real opens), the Portfolio district holds your Tool Portfolio. Trouble shows up as real incidents: a dead tool catches fire, a blocked project crashes, an overdue one sits with hazards on, heavy active work gets a crane, and a risk you're actively mitigating sends a response vehicle. Enough trouble at once and the sky turns to storm. Click anything.
